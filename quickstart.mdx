## How simple is it? 

<Steps>
  <Step title="Invite Cypher">
    Head over to [cypher.bot/invite](https://cypher.bot/invite) to invite Cypher
    to your server.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Setup and customise">
    Personalize the bot to suit your needs—configure channels, security settings, and more with ease using our documentation or the [commands page](https://cypher.bot/commands)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sit back, let Cypher take over">
    That’s it! It’s really that simple. Your server is now almost fully automated—from moderation and security to entertainment and beyond.
  </Step>
</Steps>
